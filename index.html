Welcome to SED Website!

SED is a international science club mainly for high school students.
The club is covering 6 scientific fields:

- Chemistry

- Physics/Astronomy

- Biology

- Psychology/Neuroscience

- Environmental Science

- Computer Science

SED is consisted with 3 three teams: Research Team, Magazine Team, Spreading-Idea(SI) Team.

Followings are the role of each team

Research Team

Pursuing for more convenient and more environment-friendly solutions, Research Team conducts research to maximize positive externality of science around the world in many different places.

Research Team

In Magazine Team, members tries to deepen, research, collaborate to create Magazine named "Curiosite". Magazine Team publishes Magazine once a month at the beginning of each month.

SI Team

SI Team tries to spread the scientific knowledge around the world throughout the Social-Networking-Services(SNSs), including youtube. The work of SI Team will be updated in the "Video" menu, which links to SED youtube channel.
